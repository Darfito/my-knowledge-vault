## Key insight from today
The AI team pipeline is **intentionally simpler** than real human teams — no mid-cycle back-edges (PM bouncing specs from QA, UI/UX iterating mid-implementation). This is a feature, not a limitation. Simpler chains produce more reliable outcomes from imperfect agents. Back-edges are deferred to Phase 2 as a deliberate choice.
